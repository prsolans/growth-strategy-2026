## What is Account Research?

Account Research generates a comprehensive, AI-powered account brief — grounded in your actual Docusign book of business data — and delivers it directly to you in Slack. No manual research. No digging through Salesforce. Just ask, and it arrives.

- Walk into any call with a complete picture of your account
- Surface expansion whitespace before you're in the room
- Ground your conversations in data, not assumptions

Your account brief is assembled and a link is delivered back to you in Slack. Open it, share it, use it to prep.

### Known limitations

**Prospect accounts** — If the account has no Docusign bookscrub data (e.g. a prospect), internal sections (Footprint, Account Health) will be sparse or empty. AI-estimated sections will still generate.

**GTM Group ID behavior** — Results may differ between Salesforce URL and GTM_GROUP ID inputs. If an account spans multiple GTM groups, the GTM_GROUP ID may merge or split data unexpectedly. When in doubt, use the Salesforce URL.

**AI-estimated sections** — Agreement Landscape, Contract Commerce Estimates, and Priority Map are AI-generated from public information. Output quality varies by industry; niche or complex accounts may have lower accuracy. Treat these as directional, not authoritative.

**Data freshness** — Internal data (Footprint, Account Health) reflects the most recent bookscrub refresh — not live Salesforce data. There may be a lag of days to weeks depending on the refresh cycle.
