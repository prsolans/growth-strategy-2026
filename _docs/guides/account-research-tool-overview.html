Account Research Tool

AI-generated, research-backed growth strategy documents for any Docusign customer account.

Account Plan Output

The tool produces a six-section Google Doc that gives AEs a ready-to-use baseline for account research. The document opens with current contract data — ACV, seat utilization, and envelope volume — alongside a side-by-side view of active vs. unused products, surfacing immediate upsell opportunities from internal book-of-business data. An account health scorecard follows, derived entirely from internal signals.

The second half of the main document is AI-synthesized: a narrative of the customer's strategic business initiatives and where Docusign fits, a long-term opportunity map organized by business unit with agreement intensity scoring (with a column left intentionally blank for the AE to complete), and a scored shortlist of the top three high-value "Big Bet" expansion opportunities with estimated size and ROI. Supporting research — company profile, org structure, agreement landscape, and commerce estimates — is compiled in an appendix.

Access & Infrastructure
Available through the Genius Bar
Request access or onboarding via the Genius Bar. The tool is provisioned per territory and configured with your Drive folder and API credentials before first use.
Built on internal Docusign tooling
Runs entirely within Google Workspace using the standard enterprise bookscrub sheet. AI calls route through Docusign's internal LLM infrastructure — no data leaves the corporate environment.
No data preparation required
Select an account from the sheet, click Generate, and the completed document is saved to Drive in under 5 minutes. Five AI research calls, signal analysis across 15 products, and a fully formatted 9-section executive report — the bookscrub export is the only input needed.
