Why it matters
Every section is grounded in internal Bookscrub signals + AI-powered public research — so you walk in knowing more than anyone in the room.
How it works
Takes under 5 minutes from start to link.
Step 1
Reads your internal Bookscrub data to pull usage signals, product footprint, and account health indicators.
Step 2
Runs 5 sequential AI research calls to gather public intelligence — financials, org structure, tech stack, and more.
Step 3
Assembles a 9-section Google Doc and saves it to your team's Drive folder. You get the link when it's ready.
Tips
This doc is a living asset — share it with your manager, SA, or CSM and build on it together before the meeting.
Drop it in your account Slack channel or attach it to the SFDC opportunity so the whole team is working from the same brief.
Come back to it. Re-run any time the account situation changes — each run produces a fresh doc reflecting current data.
