How it works
Takes under 5 minutes from start to link.
Step 1
Reads your internal Bookscrub data to pull usage signals, product footprint, and account health indicators.
Step 2
Runs 5 sequential AI research calls to gather public intelligence — financials, org structure, tech stack, and more.
Step 3
Assembles a 9-section Account Research brief and saves it to your team's Drive folder. You get the link when it's ready.
Tips
Your Account Research brief is a living asset — share it with your manager, SA, or CSM and build on it together before the meeting.
Drop the Account Research brief in your account Slack channel or attach it to the SFDC opportunity so the whole team is working from the same brief.
Come back to it. Re-run the Account Research brief any time the account situation changes — each run reflects the latest data.
